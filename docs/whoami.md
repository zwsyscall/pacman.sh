# whoami

Who am I? Where are you? This post serves as an answer to these questions and more!

This is a blog I set up to help me organize and manage my thoughts on various topics. Some posts will (probably) cover technology, some geopolitik and others general life stuff. Very mundane, I'm aware. The point of this website is to just let me scream into the void without cluttering up chats or shoving my thoughts down a journal that I'll never show anyone, there's \<some\> accountability to be had with public blogs I feel.

Anywho, let's move on from the meta-journaling, *who am I?* Tough question, I feel like I'm currently in some state of flux, which is rare for me. For as long as I can think back, my career choice and roughly what I want from life have been obvious. I want to do puter stuff, preferably some sort of sysadmin job and become a wizard in said job. Grow the beard, wear a purple tunic and everything that comes with it. Now, after I've "achieved" this job, I feel a hunger, an ambition for more. I want to do something more technical, more wizard-y. 

I'm not a good programmer by any means, but I like tinkering with things and reverse engineering stuff and just generally trying things out. Maybe 10 years ago I could've made a career off of that, but I am for once in my life, not optimistic about the future. I fear that automation and pesky chatbots will be the bane of what could have been a fun career. It's not like all the jobs will disappear in 5 years, but the nature of the work is ever-changing. Some of the more technical people in the field seem to be moving RE to LLMs and automatic workflows. This is probably good for society, but 'bad' for hobbyists. In a sense at least.

Nice tangent that doesn't really anwer the question of who I am. Well, in short, I'm in my early twenties, **Finnish**. I'm a full-time employed system specialist and in my free time I'm interested in reverse engineering, lower level 'shenanigans' and OSINT. I'm *not* particularly talented in any of these, but I make an active effort to better myself and learn more. I'm able to sit down and study something I'm really interested in for hours without a break, which is my 'superpower'. I also ~~own~~ live with a cat, [Jorge](https://jorge-a-day.fi/). He is my beautiful scaredy cat.

Aside from sitting at my computer, I enjoy 'marching', travelling abroad and mindfulness.
